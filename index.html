About us

The Oxford Pre-Hospital Emergency Medicine and Major Trauma Society (OxPHEM) is run by clinical medicine students in their final years of study looking to provide a platform for education and communication in pre-hospital care. We aim to attract the next generation of PHEM doctors to the specialty by arranging speakers and events for both medical students and doctors across the UK and from overseas, including a yearly two-day PHEM conference held at the John Radcliffe Hospital in Oxford.
